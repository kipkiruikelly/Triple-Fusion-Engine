Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BullLogic, predictions, and payments.
How do BullLogic predictions work?
We train machine learning models (Linear Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost ensembles) on years of historical price data and technical indicators for each supported asset. When you request a prediction, the models analyze the latest market data and output a predicted price, a direction (bullish or bearish), and a confidence score. Timeframes range from 1 minute to 1 day.
How accurate are the predictions?
It varies by asset and timeframe, and we publish it all. Every prediction on the platform is automatically graded against what the market actually did once its time horizon passes. See the live numbers on our Track Record page. Models with too few graded calls honestly show "insufficient data". No market prediction is ever guaranteed. Expect wrong calls regularly, even from a good model.
Is this financial advice?
No. BullLogic is an information and analytics tool. Nothing on the platform is a recommendation to buy or sell any asset. You are solely responsible for your trading decisions and any gains or losses. If you need personal financial advice, talk to a licensed advisor. Never trade money you cannot afford to lose.
What assets can I get predictions for?
Over 50 instruments: major US stocks (Apple, Microsoft, Tesla and more), ETFs (SPY, QQQ, GLD), crypto (BTC, ETH, SOL), forex pairs (EURUSD, GBPUSD), commodities (gold, oil, silver) and global indices (S&P 500, Nasdaq 100, FTSE, DAX, Nikkei). The full list appears in the app sidebar.
How many free predictions do I get?
Free accounts get 5 predictions per day, reset at midnight UTC. If a prediction fails because of a data problem, it is not counted against your quota. Pro accounts have no daily limit.
How do I pay with M-Pesa?
Go to Pricing, choose a plan, and enter your Safaricom number (07XX XXX XXX). You will receive an STK prompt on your phone. Enter your M-Pesa PIN to approve. Your Pro access activates instantly after Safaricom confirms the payment, and we email you a receipt with the M-Pesa reference number.
The M-Pesa prompt did not arrive or the payment failed. What now?
Common causes: phone off or unlocked screen timeout, insufficient balance, wrong PIN, or an existing M-Pesa session. Wait a minute and try again. If money left your account but Pro is not active within 10 minutes, our system re-checks pending payments automatically. Still stuck? Contact support with your M-Pesa confirmation SMS and we will resolve it.
What is your refund policy?
Subscriptions can be refunded within 48 hours of payment if you have run fewer than 10 Pro predictions in that period. Refunds are processed back to the paying M-Pesa number and may take a few business days. See the Terms of Service for full details.
Can I sign in with Google?
Yes. Use the "Continue with Google" button on the sign-in page. If you already registered with the same email address and a password, the Google sign-in links to your existing account, so you keep your history and can use either method afterwards.
I forgot my password. How do I recover my account?
Use "Forgot password?" on the sign-in page. We email a single-use reset link valid for 1 hour to your registered address. For security, resetting your password signs you out of all other devices. If you signed up with Google, just use the Google button instead.
Why must I verify my email?
Verification proves the address belongs to you, protects your account recovery path, and is required before running predictions or making payments. The link in the verification email is valid for 24 hours and you can request a new one from the "check your inbox" screen.
Where does your market data come from?
Live and historical prices come from Yahoo Finance, with Alpaca used for high-resolution (1-minute) training data. When a data source is temporarily unavailable we show the most recent cached prices with an honest banner rather than blank screens.
What do you do with my data?
We store your account details, prediction history, and payment records to run the service. We do not sell your personal data. Read the full Privacy Policy, which covers your rights under Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019.
How do I cancel or delete my account?
Pro plans purchased via M-Pesa are fixed-term (30 or 365 days) and simply do not renew unless you pay again, so there is nothing to cancel. To delete your account and data, contact support from your registered email and we will process the deletion within 30 days as required by law.
