Next-Day Price Prediction

Predicts the next trading day's closing price using six months of historical OHLCV data and 25 engineered technical features.

Three ML Models

Linear Regression, Random Forest, and LSTM are trained and compared, giving multiple perspectives on the same prediction task.

Technical Indicators

RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, and moving averages are computed automatically from live market data and displayed with each prediction.

How the System Works

Linear Regression

A statistical baseline that models the linear relationship between technical indicators and the next day's closing price.

Random Forest

An ensemble of 300 decision trees trained on technical indicators and five-day lag features, capturing non-linear price patterns.

LSTM

A deep learning model that learns from 60-day sequential price data. Designed specifically for time-series forecasting tasks.
