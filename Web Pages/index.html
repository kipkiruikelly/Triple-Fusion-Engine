How the Prediction Works

Linear Regression Baseline model. Finds a linear relationship between technical indicators and the next day's price. Fast and interpretable.
Random Forest Ensemble of 300 decision trees trained on technical indicators and 5-day lag features. Captures non-linear patterns.
LSTM Deep learning model that learns from 60-day price sequences. Designed specifically for time-series forecasting.
