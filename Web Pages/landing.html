How it works
Signal generation in three layers.
Every prediction passes through a strict multi-gate process before reaching you.
1

ICT Bias Gate

Price must be above the 200 SMA with bullish structure and EMA20 > EMA50 alignment. No gate, no trade.

2

ICT Pattern Score

Order blocks, FVGs, liquidity sweeps, PD zones and displacement are scored. Minimum score of 3 required to proceed.

3

ML Confirmation

LR and RF models must agree. Agreement adds +2 to the total score; conflict subtracts 1.

4

Signal Delivered

Total score ≥ 5 with ICT bias present fires a BULLISH or BEARISH signal with entry, SL, and TP levels.
