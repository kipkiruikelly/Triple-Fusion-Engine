Frequently asked questions

How accurate is the signal engine?
The XGBoost direction classifier achieves 47–58% out-of-sample accuracy on walk-forward cross-validation — higher than random, with AMZN reaching 58.7% AUC. See the live track record for ongoing paper-trading results.
What tickers are supported?
AAPL, MSFT, TSLA, NVDA, GOOGL, AMZN, META, QQQ, SPY, DIA, and ADBE — with Daily, 1-Hour, and 15-Minute models for each. More tickers are added regularly.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes — cancel any time from your profile page. You keep Pro access until the end of the billing period.
What are the daily email alerts?
Each market-close day, the paper trader scans all tickers for BUY/SELL signals and emails Pro subscribers with the ticker, direction, confidence, entry price, and suggested SL/TP levels.
Is this financial advice?
No. BullLogic provides algorithmic signals for educational purposes. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Always conduct your own research before trading.
