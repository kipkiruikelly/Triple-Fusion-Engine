Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026
Template notice: this policy was drafted for BullLogic and should be reviewed by a qualified lawyer before commercial launch, particularly for registration requirements with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner of Kenya.

BullLogic ("we", "us") operates a stock market prediction platform available in Kenya and internationally. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, how we protect it, and the rights you have under the Data Protection Act, 2019 (Kenya) and related regulations.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data: username, email address, and a hashed password. Passwords are stored only as salted cryptographic hashes and can never be read by us.
  • Google sign-in data: if you use "Continue with Google", we receive your Google account identifier, email address, and display name from Google. We never see your Google password.
  • Payment data: for M-Pesa payments we store the phone number used, the amount, the plan purchased, Safaricom's transaction reference, and the M-Pesa receipt number. We do not store M-Pesa PINs. Card payments are processed by Stripe; we store only Stripe's customer and subscription references, never card numbers.
  • Usage data: predictions you run, watchlists, alerts, portfolio entries, journal notes, feedback you submit, approximate activity times, and technical logs (IP address, browser type) kept for security and abuse prevention.

2. How we use your data

  • To operate your account, deliver predictions, and honor your subscription.
  • To send transactional email: verification links, password resets, payment receipts, and optional price alerts you configure.
  • To measure and publish honest model accuracy (aggregated, never identifying you).
  • To prevent fraud, enforce rate limits, and keep the service secure.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not send marketing email without your consent.

3. Third parties involved

  • Google LLC: optional sign-in (OAuth 2.0). Subject to Google's own privacy policy.
  • Safaricom PLC (M-Pesa / Daraja API): payment processing in Kenya.
  • Stripe, Inc.: optional card payment processing.
  • Yahoo Finance and Alpaca Markets: market data sources. Your ticker requests are sent to these services without your identity attached.
  • Microsoft Azure: encrypted storage of trained model files.
  • Email delivery provider (SMTP): transactional email delivery only.

4. Storage and security

Data is stored in an encrypted-at-rest database on servers we control, reachable only over HTTPS. Access is restricted to authorized administrators, every administrative action is logged in an immutable audit trail, and admin sessions expire automatically. Backups are retained for disaster recovery.

5. Your rights (Data Protection Act, 2019)

  • Right to be informed about how your data is used (this policy).
  • Right of access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: ask us to delete your account and personal data.
  • Right to object to or restrict processing, and to data portability where applicable.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC), Kenya.

To exercise any right, contact us from your registered email address. We respond within 30 days.

6. Retention

Account data is kept while your account is active. Payment records are retained for 7 years to meet tax and audit obligations. Security logs are kept for up to 12 months. When you delete your account we remove or anonymize personal data not subject to a legal retention duty.

7. Children

BullLogic is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes

We will post any changes to this policy here and update the date above. Material changes will be announced in the app.
