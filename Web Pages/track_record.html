Methodology. When a prediction's horizon elapses (e.g. one trading day for daily predictions), the platform fetches the actual market price and records whether the predicted direction was correct and how far the price target missed, in percent. Grading runs automatically in the background for every user's predictions, including the ones that were wrong. Accuracy shown is directional (did the market move the way the model said), not a promise of profit. Past performance does not guarantee future results. This is not financial advice.
