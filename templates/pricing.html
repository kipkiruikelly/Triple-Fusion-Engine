Frequently asked questions

How accurate is the signal engine?
Live predictions come from Linear Regression + Random Forest voting (with an LSTM vote on AAPL daily), with confidence derived from vote agreement. Every prediction is stored and graded against the realized price - see the public, unedited track record for real accuracy over time rather than a backtested claim.
What tickers are supported?
76+ tickers across equities, ETFs, indices, crypto, forex, and commodities, with models for up to 8 timeframes each (1-minute through weekly, coverage varies slightly by asset's data availability). More are added regularly.
What's the difference between Plus and Pro?
Plus removes the daily prediction cap and unlocks the multi-timeframe confluence signal and a limited backtester. Pro adds everything that talks to a broker or an external system: unlimited backtesting, REST API access, and MT5 connectivity.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, cancel any time from your profile page. You keep your plan's access until the end of the billing period.
Is this financial advice?
No. BullLogic provides algorithmic signals for educational purposes. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Always conduct your own research before trading.
